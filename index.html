About Wendy

I'm an incoming Neuroscience PhD student at the University of Southern California, starting Fall 2021. I completed my MS in Computer Science at the University of Pennsylvania where I worked at Dr. Li Shen's lab as a graduate researcher.
For more info, check out my Notion CV page and my latest projects.
Research Interest
I'm interested in developing and applying machine learning methods to high dimensional or multimodal neuroimaging genetics data and study how they can inform the understanding, diagnosis or treatment of neurodegenerative disease. I also enjoy web programming and learning about distributed systems design.

About Wendy

I'm a second year master student in the Computer and Information Science (CIS) department at University of Pennsylvania, and am currently working in Dr. Li Shen's lab as a graduate researcher. I will be starting my PhD in the Neuroscience Graduate Program (NGP) at University of Southern California this fall.
I'm interested in developing and applying machine learning methods to high dimensional or multimodal neuroimaging genetics data and study how they can inform the understanding, diagnosis or treatment of neurodegenerative disease. I also enjoy web programming and learning about distributed systems design. Check out my latest projects here!

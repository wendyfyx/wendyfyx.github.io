About Wendy

[wendyfyx at seas dot upenn dot edu]
I'm a second year master student in the Computer and Information Science (CIS) department at University of Pennsylvania, and am currently working in Dr. Li Shen's lab as a graduate researcher. I will be starting my PhD in the Neuroscience Graduate Program (NGP) at University of Southern California this fall.
I'm interested in developing and applying machine learning methods for high dimensional or multimodal neuroimaging genetics data and study how they can inform the understanding, diagnosis or treatment of neurodegenerative disease with a genetic basis.
